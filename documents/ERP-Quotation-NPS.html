1 Executive Summary

This document presents the quotation for a custom-built, cloud-based ERP system designed exclusively for the piping and tubular trading industry. The system covers the complete business lifecycle from customer inquiry to dispatch, including specialized features like pipe specification management, heat number traceability, Material Test Certificate (MTC) management, and GST-compliant e-invoicing.

Unlike generic ERP solutions (TranZact, Vyapar, Tally), this system is purpose-built for piping companies with industry-specific workflows, document formats, and quality management processes that off-the-shelf software simply does not provide.

Key Value Proposition: A complete ERP system tailored for piping & tubular trading at a fraction of the cost of SAP/Oracle, with features that no generic ERP offers — pipe spec masters, flange sizing, heat traceability, MTC management, third-party inspection workflows, and complete dispatch dossier generation.

8 Terms & Conditions

8.1 Payment Terms

  1. One-time software delivery fee of Rs 60,000/- is payable upon acceptance of this quotation.
  2. AMC charges of Rs 8,000/month are payable monthly in advance, starting from the second month of go-live.
  3. All amounts are exclusive of GST (18% applicable).
  4. Enhancement requests will be estimated and billed separately upon client approval.
  5. Payment mode: Bank Transfer / UPI. Invoice will be raised prior to payment.

8.2 Software Licensing

  1. This is a software license agreement, not a source code sale. The source code remains the intellectual property of the developer.
  2. The client is granted an unlimited, non-transferable license to use the software for their business operations.
  3. The license covers unlimited users within the client's organization.
  4. The software may not be resold, sublicensed, or provided to third parties.
  5. Source code handover, if required, will be quoted separately.

8.3 Support & SLA

  1. First month: Complimentary support post go-live (bug fixes, setup assistance, training).
  2. Response time: Critical issues within 4 hours, non-critical within 24 hours (business days).
  3. Support hours: Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST.
  4. Uptime target: 99.5% monthly uptime for cloud-hosted infrastructure.
  5. Backup: Daily automated backups with 30-day retention.

8.4 Hosting & Infrastructure

  1. The system will be hosted on cloud infrastructure (Vercel / AWS) managed by the developer.
  2. Hosting costs are included in the AMC for standard usage. Excessive data/traffic beyond normal operations may incur additional charges (communicated in advance).
  3. Domain and SSL certificate management is included.
  4. The client is responsible for providing a stable internet connection for system access.

8.5 Quotation Validity

  1. This quotation is valid for 30 days from the date of issue (27th March, 2026).
  2. Prices are subject to revision after the validity period.
  3. AMC rates may be revised annually with 30 days prior notice.

8.6 Confidentiality

  1. All business data entered into the system remains the exclusive property of the client.
  2. The developer shall maintain strict confidentiality of all client data.
  3. Data will not be shared with any third party without written consent.
  4. Upon termination, client data will be exported and provided, then securely deleted from servers.

8.7 Termination

  1. Either party may terminate the AMC with 30 days written notice.
  2. Upon AMC termination, the developer is not liable for bug fixes, updates, or server management.
  3. The one-time software delivery fee is non-refundable once the system is delivered and accepted.
