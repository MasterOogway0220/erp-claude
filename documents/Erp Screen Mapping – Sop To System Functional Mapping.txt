ERP SCREEN MAPPING DOCUMENT
SOP to ERP Functional & Screen-Level Mapping
(ISO 9001:2018 Compliant – Oil, Gas & Petrochemical Trading ERP)

1. Purpose of This Document
This document maps each SOP step to specific ERP screens, functions, inputs, outputs, and controls. It is intended for: - ERP software developers - ERP implementation partners - UAT (User Acceptance Testing) - ISO 9001 auditors (objective evidence of process control)
