2. Purpose of the Document
This document defines business, functional, and compliance requirements for development or implementation of an ERP system for a trading organization supplying materials and equipment to Oil, Gas, Power & Petrochemical industries. It also ensures full compliance with ISO 9001:2018.

6. Functional Requirements (FRD – Module Wise)
6.1 Quotation Management Module
Inputs: Enquiry, customer requirements Processes: Costing, margin calculation, approval Outputs: Approved quotation (PDF/email) Controls: Version control, validity, approval ISO Reference: Clause 8.2

6.2 Inventory Management Module
Inputs: Incoming material Processes: Heat-wise stock, inspection status Outputs: Stock reports, traceability Controls: FIFO, preservation ISO Reference: Clause 8.5.2, 8.5.4

6.3 Sales Order Management Module
Inputs: Approved quotation / Customer PO Processes: Reservation, delivery scheduling Outputs: SO, dispatch notes ISO Reference: Clause 8.5

6.4 Purchase Order Management Module
Inputs: SO / Purchase requisition Processes: Vendor selection, PO issue Outputs: Approved PO ISO Reference: Clause 8.4

6.5 Quality Control & MTC Module
Inputs: Incoming material, mill certificates Processes: Inspection, acceptance/rejection Outputs: Inspection report, MTC Controls: NCR, corrective action ISO Reference: Clause 8.6, 8.7

6.6 Invoice & Payment Module
Inputs: Dispatch data Processes: Tax calculation, receipt entry Outputs: Invoice, ageing report ISO Reference: Clause 8.5.1

6.7 MIS & Dashboard Module
Inputs: Transactional data Outputs: KPIs, trends, reports ISO Reference: Clause 9.1, 9.3
